Privacy

We are devoted to safeguarding the privacy of our website visitors. The only private information we collect and store relates to demands for further data made on our contact page. Periodically, we may also compile anonymous cumulative data to better understand user performances on our site – for instance, to know top search terms. We do not give, sell or transfer this information to any third party.If we do not come into an on-going business affiliation with you or your organization, your data will be erased from our records within a period of twelve months. You can, however, request that your information be deleted at any given time.
