Who We Are

H&C's successful track record, built over a period of years, is thanks to our team of specialized senior consultants. With extensive hands-on and senior management know-how, gained working in organizations in almost every field of business, their profound knowledge and real-life expertise enables us to provide real results quickly and efficiently.
However, understanding what it's like to work in your company and overcome the challenges you face, all our consultants are specialists at revealing the real details, and getting to the root causes, of a situation.As we embrace the need to adapt and evolve, we recognize the strains change can have on a company, on its roles, relationships and networks, that can impact the desired results.
Working in small teams we'll get close to your people, involve hearts and minds, and transfer the abilities they'll need to withstand lasting change long after we leave. As part ofour partnered approach, we'll agree on clear objectives, a finite period andcost before we initiate any project.
We've found this approach results in satisfied clients, which is why the majority of our business comes from working with clients time and again.
